Max Temperature


In this analysis of latitude of a city compared to its temperature we see that the closer the city is to the equater (latitude = 0), the higher the max temperature will be. What is interesting to note, is that at a latitude of 30o, the temperature is the highest. This could be due to the angle at which Earth orbits the Sun.
